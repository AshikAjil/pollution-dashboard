We propose an AI + IoT based Clean Air Dashboard for Delhi-NCR that will:

  • Deploy low-cost IoT AQI sensors across all zones.
  • Collect real-time AQI, weather, and traffic data.
  • Use AI/ML to identify pollution sources & forecast spikes.
  • Visualize hotspots on an interactive map with alerts.
  • Help policymakers and citizens take preventive action.

ü§ñ AI & ML Models

The intelligence of the system is powered by AI/ML models:

  • Source Identification: Random Forest & XGBoost to classify pollution sources (traffic, industries, crop burning, etc.).
  • Forecasting: LSTM (deep learning) & ARIMA for time-series AQI prediction.
  • Clustering: K-means & DBSCAN to detect new pollution hotspots dynamically.
  • Anomaly Detection: Isolation Forest to catch sudden, abnormal AQI spikes.
